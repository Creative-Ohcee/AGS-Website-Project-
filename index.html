We use technology to support small-scale farmers in Africa by connecting
them to local and international markets for their produce, with the goal
of promoting food security for everyone.

The platform

AGS is a software platform that brings together all stakeholders in the agricultural supply chain, allowing them make better-informed decisions, eliminate unnecessary paperwork and dockets, reduce supply chain inefficiency and risk, open markets and increase their bottom line, all on one easy-to-use platform.

supply chain software solution.
